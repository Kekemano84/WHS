Offline mode

WHS offline trial is ready

You are offline, but cached WHS pages can still open from this device after they have been visited once online.

How to test it

Open the pages you need once while online, then add WHS to your phone home screen. After that, those pages can reopen without Render while offline.

Server actions such as login, cloud save, email and some exports still need internet. Local drafts can work on the phone.
